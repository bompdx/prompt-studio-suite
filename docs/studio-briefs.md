# Studio Briefs

One section per studio. Coverage lists are functional jobs the studio
must handle, drawn from research on what each market needs. Write every
title, description, field, task, rule, and output in your own words.
Don't copy phrasing from any source product. Aim titles at the artifact
or decision ("Ad Angle Bench", "Cart Recovery Sequence"), not the vibe.

Suite-wide profile pattern (adapt labels to the market):
1. What you sell and who buys it (textarea, required)
2. Your audience in their own words (textarea)
3. The offer or project this studio is working on right now (textarea)
4. Your main goal for the next 90 days (text)
5. Anything the AI should know about voice or constraints (text)
